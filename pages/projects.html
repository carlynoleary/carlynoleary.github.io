Wine, Dine & Talk Money

Wine, Dine, & Talk Money is a women-led startup reimagining how the financial industry engages the fastest-growing segment of wealth holders: women. Co-founded by a mother-daughter team - Deborah McLean and Carlyn O'Leary, WDTM is designed to meet women where they are - literally and figuratively.

Hosted in living rooms and other familiar spaces, WDTM offers a radically approachable model—one that builds enduring relationships and drives meaningful engagement by returning to a timeless tradition: women coming together in trusted circles to exchange knowledge and support one another.

WDTM creates the space for trust, connection, and candid conversation at every event by connecting vetted female financial advisors and experts with financially-curious women looking to host an event with her friends. There, women are free to ask questions, learn from each other, and connect over shared experience in a judgment free way.

We believe financial conversations don't have to feel intimidating—they should be empowering, and fun. Wine, Dine, and Talk Money offers women an entirely new way to talk more openly about money, access the information and education they want, and take control of their financial futures.
