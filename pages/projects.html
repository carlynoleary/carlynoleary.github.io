Fluen$y

Fluen$y is a women-led startup reimagining how the financial industry engages the fastest-growing segment of wealth holders: women. Co-founded by a mother-daughter team - Deborah McLean and Carlyn O'Leary, Fluen$y is designed to meet women where they are - literally and figuratively.

Hosted in living rooms and other familiar spaces, Fluen$y offers a radically approachable model—one that builds enduring relationships and drives meaningful engagement by returning to a timeless tradition: women coming together in trusted circles to exchange knowledge and support one another.

Fluen$y creates the space for trust, connection, and candid conversation at every event by connecting vetted female financial advisors and experts with financially-curious women looking to host an event with her friends. There, women are free to ask questions, learn from each other, and connect over shared experience in a judgment free way.

We believe financial conversations don't have to feel intimidating—they should be empowering, and fun. Fluen$y offers women an entirely new way to talk more openly about money, access the information and education they want, and take control of their financial futures.
